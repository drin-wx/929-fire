General Order on the Temporary Prohibition of Boarding BIFRA-Affiliated Fire Trucks.

READ MORE

Effective immediately (27th of May 2026), all Officers, Members, and Probies of 929 Fire and Rescue Volunteer are temporarily prohibited from boarding or riding any fire trucks owned by the Barangays of Biñan, Laguna or by volunteer brigades affiliated with or recognized under BIFRA. This directive is in accordance with the order and advice issued by BIFRA. Therefore, no member, officer, or probie of 929 Fire and Rescue Volunteer shall board any affiliated fire apparatus unless otherwise authorized.
