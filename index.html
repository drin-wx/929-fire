Leading the way in
Emergency Response.

The 929 Fire and Rescue Volunteer is a dedicated non-profit group committed to safeguarding lives and properties through prompt emergency response, community education, and proactive disaster preparedness.

OUR MISSION

To serve our community with the highest level of care, protection, and education by providing timely and efficient emergency response and proactive safety awareness.

OUR VISION

To be a leading and trusted emergency service provider in the Philippines, driven by innovation, dedication, and excellence in public safety and education.


AUGUSTO V. ALPASAN

Founder

recognized as the father of 929 volunteers, exemplified dedication through every challenge and bravery he faced. He risked everything to save lives during disasters, earning him the title of a hero.

Now, his son, Aliant Anthony M. Alpasan, continues the legacy initiated by his father, ensuring that the 929 Fire and Rescue Volunteer and Radio Communication Organization remains a successful group dedicated to assisting our fellow citizens for free. Even in the absence of the father of 929, the organization continues to thrive, carrying on the mission that was started.
