PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: 24 June 2026

1. Introduction

The 929 Fire and Rescue Volunteer Radio Communication Group Inc. values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our official website, emergency platform systems, or when registering as an emergency responder or donor.

2. Information We Collect

To provide effective disaster communication and response management, we may collect the following data:


• Personal Identification: Name, contact details, address, and blood type (for responder records).

• Incident & Dispatch Logs: Emergency reports, location coordinates, and radio traffic details processed via our dispatcher system.

• Technical Data: IP addresses and basic device telemetry to ensure security and prevent unauthorized access.

3. How We Use Your Data

All information collected is used solely for non-profit and humanitarian operations, specifically for:


• Coordinating emergency dispatch with local agencies (e.g., BFP and rescue units).

• Verifying volunteer memberships and responder authenticity.

• Ensuring stable performance of community web systems like CabuyaoKonek and LagunaWISE.

4. Data Security & Protection

In accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) of the Philippines, we enforce strict security protocols to safeguard your records. We do not sell, rent, or lease your private database metrics to third-party commercial advertising operations.

Contact Privacy Team
