Why Your Support Matters

As a purely non-profit, non-governmental emergency institution, the 929 Fire and Rescue Volunteer - Radio Communication Group Inc. functions entirely through volunteer dedication and community generosity. Every contribution directly funds the infrastructure needed to protect our local communities for free.

1. Web Infrastructure & Domain Renewal

Donations cover web domain subscriptions and secure database server layers required during rapid alert monitoring.

2. Disaster Radio & Dispatch Maintenance

Maintaining active walkie-talkies, stationary base antennas, power supplies, and automated tracking modules requires consistent hardware upkeep. Keeping lines active means we can dispatch teams instantly when calls arrive.

3. Free Community Emergency Response

We do not charge citizens for rescue actions, emergency medical services (EMS), or disaster safety training. Financial contributions keep our trucks fueled, medical equipment stocked, and systems updated.
